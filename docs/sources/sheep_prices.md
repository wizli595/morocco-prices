## KEY EVENTS TIMELINE

- **1970**: Sheep meat 7.80 DH/kg. Morocco had 15.7M sheep
- **1985**: Meat jumped to 32 DH/kg (+310% in 15 years)
- **1991-2006**: FAOSTAT has complete annual data. Price range 39-56.50 DH/kg
- **1998**: 51.25 DH/kg confirmed by both FAOSTAT and INRA
- **2007-2021**: FAOSTAT GAP - only price index available, no actual DH prices
- **2020**: COVID dip - prices dropped ~10%, herders lost 300-500 DH/animal
- **2021-2024**: Six consecutive drought years. Feed costs up 60-100%
- **2022**: Ukraine war spiked grain/feed prices globally
- **2023**: HCP reported meat prices +21% since 2019, +15.9% YoY
- **2024**: Average sheep at Eid hit ~6,500 DH (vs ~3,000 in 2005)
- **2025**: King Mohammed VI asked nation to skip Eid sacrifice (first since 1996). Herds down 38%
- **2026**: Recovery - supply 8-9M head, prices moderating. Eid on May 27
