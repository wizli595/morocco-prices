# Product Catalog

## Overview

~500+ products across 18 categories. Covers everything tracked by Morocco's HCP CPI basket (478 articles), plus agricultural exports and livestock.

Products marked with **[S]** are subsidized by the Caisse de Compensation.
Products marked with **[P]** are politically sensitive (price changes make headlines).
Products marked with **[E]** are major exports.

## Product ID Convention

Format: `{CATEGORY}-{SUBCATEGORY}-{PRODUCT}-{VARIETY}`

Examples:
```
MEAT-SHEEP                     # Generic sheep meat
MEAT-SHEEP-SARDI               # Sardi breed specifically
DAIRY-MILK-UHT                 # UHT milk
DAIRY-MILK-RAW                 # Raw milk
CEREAL-WHEAT-SOFT              # Soft wheat
FLOUR-NATIONALE                # Subsidized national flour
BREAD-KHOBZ                    # Traditional round bread
ENERGY-BUTANE-12KG             # 12kg butane cylinder
FISH-SARDINE-FRESH             # Fresh sardines
FISH-SARDINE-CANNED            # Canned sardines
VEG-TOMATO                     # Tomatoes
VEG-TOMATO-PASTE               # Tomato concentrate
FRUIT-ORANGE                   # Oranges
FRUIT-CLEMENTINE               # Clementines
SPICE-SAFFRON                  # Saffron
LIVESTOCK-SHEEP-SARDI          # Live Sardi sheep
FEED-BARLEY                    # Feed-grade barley
HOUSING-RENT-APT-1BR-CENTER    # 1BR apartment rent, city center
```

## HCP IPC Basket Alignment

The HCP CPI basket (base 2017) contains **478 articles** across **1,391 product varieties**, organized into 12 COICOP divisions:

| Division | Name | Weight (%) | Our Coverage |
|----------|------|-----------|-------------|
| 01 | Food & non-alcoholic beverages | ~38% | Categories 1-13 |
| 02 | Alcoholic beverages & tobacco | ~2% | Category 20 |
| 03 | Clothing & footwear | ~4% | Not prioritized |
| 04 | Housing, water, electricity, gas | ~14% | Categories 16-17 |
| 05 | Furnishings & household equipment | ~4% | Not prioritized |
| 06 | Health | ~5% | Not prioritized |
| 07 | Transport | ~12% | Category 19 (partial) |
| 08 | Communication | ~4% | Category 19 (telecoms) |
| 09 | Recreation & culture | ~3% | Not prioritized |
| 10 | Education | ~6% | Not prioritized |
| 11 | Restaurants & hotels | ~4% | Category 19 (food services) |
| 12 | Miscellaneous goods & services | ~4% | Not prioritized |

**Priority**: Divisions 01, 04, 07 cover ~64% of the CPI weight and are fully covered by our catalog.
